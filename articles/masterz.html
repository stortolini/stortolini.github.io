Abstract

Optimizing the life-cycle of plastic products, within this circular economy model, reduces the input of pollutant material into the environment, consequently reducing the impact of the individual community's so-called "Plastic Footprint," while, with the use of Blockchain technology, public involvement in plastic collection and recycling is aided, as each user will be credited with a return in WasteWise Token (WWT), a utility token built on the Celo chain.

It will be possible to collect WWT, for example, by placing plastic or other recyclables at WasteWise network collection points. Depending on the type and amount of material recycled, you will receive an amount of WWT directly into your wallet (virtual wallet). It will be possible to convert the WasteWise Tokens collected into any cryptocurrency supported by the Celo chain at any time, directly from the WasteWise centralized application.

Recycling Spots and the eco-sustainable market guarantee circularity and high activity in the Blockchain network, with numerous transactions day by day, while the success of the project guarantees an excellent investment opportunity, as it results in an increase in the value of a single token, a stimulus for "holding" activities of large WWT capitals and for mining operations (eco-sustainable, without electricity consumption).
