With up to 98% of companies recognizing the importance of digital transformation and innovation—and 90% actively investing in it—the demand for professionals who can bridge IT and business strategies has never been higher. Unfortunately, only 53% of businesses feel equipped to manage this transformation effectively, largely due to a lack of professionals who possess expertise in both areas.

This is where the DTM program excels: it trains Digital Transformation Managers who use cutting-edge technologies to enhance business efficiency and effectiveness, all while aligning with strategic business plans.

My Experience: An Interdisciplinary Approach

  • Unlike traditional Data Science courses, the DTM program incorporates essential business topics like Innovation Strategy, Finance, and Business Models, which are key to driving transformation in any organization.
  • Compared to standard Business programs, the DTM course equipped me with new IT skills as well as emerging technologies like Machine Learning, Big Data, and Cloud Platforms. While most of my Bachelor's knowledge was sufficient, these advanced courses pushed me further—because "you never stop learning"!

This program gave me the perfect combination of technical expertise and business acumen, preparing me to take on a pivotal role in the exciting world of digital transformation.
